What is Guam?

Guam, island and unincorporated territory of the United States in the North Pacific Ocean, the largest, most populous, and southernmost of the Mariana Islands.

  • Hagåtña (Agana) is the capital.
  • Major settlements are Dededo, in the north-central part of the island, Machanao, in the north, and Apotgan, on the west coast.
